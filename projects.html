Email Dual Factor Authentication

In a world of cyber threats, a new form of security called dual-factor authentication emerged. According to a survey conducted by Google, almost 45% of Google users have some sort of dual authentication enabled on their accounts. This is why I chose this to be a project of mine.

Goals

  • Incorporate Actix, one of Rust's premier web frameworks.
  • Get used to larger projects in Rust.
  • Learn how a service like email authentication works.
